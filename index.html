What YOU Can Do

Step 1: Sign up for Weed Warrior Training!

Submit the sign-up form on this page, to be notified of when our next class is, so you can start volunteering!


Step 2: Attend a class!

You'll learn how to identify invasive plant species, safety precautions, and plant removal methods, plus fun and exciting facts and history of the MPEA!


Step 3: Start helping out!

Imbued with your new knowledge, empowered with your very own personalized Weed Warrior Volunteer membership card, and a snazzy volunteer shirt, you can join volunteer plant removal events, or schedule your own time to come and improve the health of the MPEA for all to enjoy!
